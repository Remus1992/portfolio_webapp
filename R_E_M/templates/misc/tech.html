Technology & Equipment

I’ve seen a lot of “What’s in my camera bag” videos online over the past five years which, from my perspective as a self-taught filmmaker, have always proved extremely helpful. They generally would do two things for me: 1) they brought a piece of equipment to my attention that I didn’t know existed or 2) they would recommend a single brand out of the intimidating Ocean of options. For those reasons, I’ve decided to post a detailed list of my equipment with the hope that it may benefit others.


At the moment, my website is in its infancy. As such, this page is pretty barren, but I intend to update it with a few of my thoughts and views on what I've used so far when I have the time. I might even write a blog posting on some if necessary.
