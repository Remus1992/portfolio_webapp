Technology

I’ve seen a lot of “What’s in my camera bag” videos online over the past five years which, from my perspective as a self-taught filmmaker, have always proved extremely helpful. They generally would do two things for me: 1) bring to my attention a piece of equipment I didn’t know existed or 2) recommend a single brand out of the imitidating Ocean of options. For those reasons, I’ve decided to post a detailed list of my equipment with the hope that it may benefit others.
